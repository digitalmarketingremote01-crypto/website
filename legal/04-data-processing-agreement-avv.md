## 1. Subject & duration
The Processor processes personal data **only to provide the agreed performance-marketing services** (Google Ads, Meta Ads, conversion tracking & analytics, reporting). Duration = the term of the underlying service agreement.

## 2. Nature & purpose; data types; data subjects
See **Annex 1**. In summary:
- **Purpose:** campaign management, conversion tracking, audience/analytics, reporting.
- **Personal data:** online identifiers (cookie/pixel IDs, device/IP data), website-behaviour & conversion events, audience/remarketing data, and any contact data the Controller shares (e.g. for customer-match lists).
- **Data subjects:** the Controller's website visitors, leads and customers.
- **Special-category data:** none processed (the Controller shall not provide special-category data).

## 3. Controller's rights & instructions
The Processor processes personal data **only on the Controller's documented instructions** (this AVV + the service agreement being the initial instruction). The Processor informs the Controller if an instruction appears to infringe data-protection law.

## 4. Processor obligations (Art. 28(3))
The Processor shall:
- (a) process only on documented instructions, including for transfers;
- (b) ensure persons authorised to process are bound by **confidentiality**;
- (c) implement appropriate **technical & organisational measures (Art. 32)** — see **Annex 2**;
- (d) engage **sub-processors** only under Clause 6;
- (e) **assist the Controller** (by appropriate measures) in responding to **data-subject requests** (Art. 12–23);
- (f) assist the Controller with **Art. 32–36** obligations (security, breach notification, DPIAs);
- (g) at the Controller's choice, **delete or return** all personal data at the end of the services and delete existing copies (unless storage is legally required);
- (h) make available information needed to demonstrate compliance and **allow for and contribute to audits**.

## 5. Personal-data breach
The Processor notifies the Controller **without undue delay (and within `[48]` hours)** after becoming aware of a personal-data breach, with the information the Controller needs to meet its Art. 33/34 duties.

## 6. Sub-processors
The Controller grants **general authorisation** for the sub-processors listed in **Annex 3** (e.g. Google, Meta, Google Analytics/Tag Manager, hosting/email forwarding). The Processor will inform the Controller of intended changes and give the Controller the opportunity to **object**. The Processor imposes data-protection obligations on each sub-processor equivalent to this AVV.
