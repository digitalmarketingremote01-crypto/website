## 1. Definitions
**"Protected Clients"** means any client, customer or prospect of the Partner that the Provider becomes aware of, is given access to, or works on, in connection with the partnership — including the Partner's End Clients and any leads/prospects disclosed by the Partner.

## 2. The Provider's commitment
For the **duration of the partnership and for `[24]` months after it ends**, the Provider shall **not**, whether directly or indirectly, on its own account or for any third party:
1. **contact, approach or solicit** any Protected Client;
2. **accept, take on, or provide services** to any Protected Client outside of, or in competition with, the Partner;
3. **poach, divert, or attempt to divert** any Protected Client away from the Partner; or
4. **disclose** the identity of any Protected Client to third parties.

The Provider will remain **invisible in the background**. All communication with Protected Clients runs **through the Partner**, unless the Partner requests otherwise in writing.

## 3. Exceptions
This Agreement does **not** restrict the Provider from serving a business that it can demonstrably show (by written records) was **already its own client before**, or that approaches the Provider **wholly independently** without any link to the partnership or the Partner's disclosure.

## 4. Confidentiality of client data
The Provider treats all Protected Client information as strictly confidential and uses it solely to deliver the agreed white-label services to the Partner.
