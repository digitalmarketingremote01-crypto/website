# Digital Marketing Remote — Contract Templates

Five working contract templates for DMR, in **English**, under **German law / German courts**, for a **B2B-only** business (§ 14 BGB).

> ⚠️ **These are templates, not legal advice.** They are accurate to your business model and a strong starting point — but **have a qualified German/EU lawyer review them before you use them with real clients**, especially Document 04 (data protection / international transfer). They are **private documents for you** — they are **not** part of the website and are **not** deployed.

---

## The documents

| # | Document | Use it when… |
|---|---|---|
| 01 | **Direct-Client Service Agreement** | You sign a **direct business client** (this replaces the "B2C contract" — your clients are businesses, not consumers). |
| 02 | **White-Label Partner Agreement** | You sign an **agency partner** you fulfil for in the background. |
| 03 | **Non-Solicitation / Client-Protection Agreement** | Every agency partnership — this is the **"signed promise not to take their clients"** your /partner page now advertises. Goes with Doc 02. |
| 04 | **Data Processing Agreement (AVV)** | With **every** client/partner — Art. 28 GDPR. Your site already promises this. **Highest legal-review priority.** |
| 05 | **Mutual NDA** | Before sharing account access, audits, pricing or pitch material with a prospect/partner. |

**Typical bundles**
- New direct client → **01 + 04** (and 05 if you exchange sensitive info first).
- New agency partner → **02 + 03 + 04** (and 05 first).

## What these are built on (your real terms)
- Provider: Digital Marketing Remote, sole proprietorship, Lahore, Pakistan — **no DE/AT/CH establishment**.
- **B2B only** (§ 14 BGB); **reverse-charge VAT** (§ 13b UStG), net prices.
- Services: Google Ads, Meta Ads, tracking & analytics, reporting.
- **Monthly-cancellable, no minimum term, no upfront cost.**
- **Performance guarantee** = free 2nd month if jointly-defined KPIs are missed in month 1 (waived fee, **no cash payout**).
- **Client funds 100% of the ad budget directly**; DMR takes no margin on media spend.
- Client owns the ad accounts; DMR works on **access rights only**.

---

## Built to protect you — and flexible on price
Drafted **provider-favourable (your side first):** fees **payable in advance** and **non-refundable** once the month starts; right to **suspend on late payment** + default interest (§ 288 BGB); **immediate termination** for non-payment or breach; **client/partner indemnity** for their own products, ads and policy breaches; **liability capped** at the last `[3]` months' fees; **force majeure**; right to **decline prohibited/high-risk work**.

**Rates and terms stay negotiable per client.** Nothing commercial is locked into the contract body — scope, fees and conditions are set **per deal in the Annex / Order Form** and reflect what you agree in discussion. You can also **raise future-month fees with `[30]` days' notice**. So you quote each client differently without ever rewriting the contract.

## Blanks you must fill in (search for `[ ]`)
- Client/partner legal name, address, register/VAT-ID, signatory.
- Fees / tiers / wholesale prices (Annex A in 01 and 02).
- KPIs for the performance guarantee (01, Annex A).
- Notice periods (default `[14]` days) and liability cap window (default `[3]` months).
- **Court venue** — pick one German city (e.g. Berlin) for the jurisdiction clause.
- **Doc 03:** the contractual-penalty amount and the post-term duration (default 24 months) — **lawyer must confirm these are enforceable**.
- **Doc 04:** finalise the **SCC module + annexes + Transfer Impact Assessment** (Pakistan = third country) and the **sub-processor list** and **TOMs**.

---

## Three things to get a lawyer's eyes on first
1. **Doc 04 — international data transfer (Pakistan → EU clients).** Needs EU Standard Contractual Clauses + a transfer assessment. This is the real compliance risk.
2. **Doc 03 — the non-solicitation penalty and 24-month post-term restriction.** German courts scrutinise contractual penalties and overly long restraints.
3. **Jurisdiction clause** — "German courts" is great for client trust; confirm the exact venue wording with a lawyer (you have no German seat, and AT/CH clients sit outside Germany).
