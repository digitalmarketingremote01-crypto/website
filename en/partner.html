Agency FAQs

What partners ask most

Will you contact or poach our clients?
Never — and we put it in writing. Every partnership includes a signed non-solicitation agreement: we will not contact, solicit or work directly with your clients. We stay invisible in the background — your client stays your client.
Who owns the ad accounts?
Your client's, or yours. We work with access rights only and never take ownership, so you keep full control of all data and assets.
How is the reporting delivered?
Reports are delivered neutral and unbranded — no DMR name. You present them as your own work.
What do you handle?
Google Ads and Meta Ads: strategy, campaign build, ongoing management, tracking and analytics setup, and full reporting. On request we also support you with audits and pitch material to win new clients.
Is there a minimum term?
We offer flexible partner terms with no rigid minimum. Scope and terms are set individually based on your volume.
How do you handle our clients' data?
All campaign data stays in your clients' accounts — we work only with the necessary access rights. You keep full control at all times.
What does the partnership cost?
Terms are individual, based on volume and scope — priced partner-friendly so your margin stays protected. We discuss concrete terms in a no-obligation partner call.
